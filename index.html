Practice Penguins - Privacy Policy & Data Deletion

Last updated: September 22, 2025

Privacy Policy

This is a placeholder privacy policy for the beta testing phase of the Practice Penguins application. A full, detailed privacy policy will be available prior to the public launch.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. No personal data is shared with third parties, except as required for the app's core functionality (e.g., authentication services).

Account and Data Deletion Policy for Practice Penguins

As a user of the Practice Penguins application, you have the right to request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion:

  1. Compose an email from the email address associated with your Practice Penguins account for verification purposes.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. Send the email to: info@practicepenguins.app

We will process your request and confirm the deletion via email within 30 days.

Data That Will Be Deleted:

Upon a valid request, we will permanently delete the following types of data associated with your account:

  • Account Credentials: Your email address, password information, and unique Firebase User ID.
  • User-Provided Information: Your display name and any uploaded profile picture.
  • App Activity: All practice logs, saved practice goals, earned sticker data, and weekly progress reports.
  • User Content: Any notes or messages created within the app, such as lesson notes from a teacher.

No user data is retained after the deletion process is complete.
